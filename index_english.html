MyGreenPlus Kitchen Oil Recovery System provides complete used cooking oil solutions for commercial kitchens, turning waste into extra income while solving drainage issues and improving operational efficiency.

Business Opportunity

Market Size & Potential

Malaysia generates over 100,000 tons of used cooking oil annually from commercial kitchens. With government regulations tightening on waste disposal, the demand for professional UCO collection services is growing rapidly.

Key Drivers:

  • Government environmental regulations
  • Restaurant compliance requirements
  • Biodiesel industry demand
  • Circular economy initiatives

  • Market Distribution - Biodiesel feedstock
